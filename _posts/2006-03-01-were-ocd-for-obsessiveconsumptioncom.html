Have you heard of ObsessiveConsumption.com?

It's pretty sweet!

Among the many things this girl, Kate Bingaman, does is make drawings of things she buys. She also draws her monthly credit card bills and sews these nifty dollar-shaped pillows. Also, she takes cool pictures of esoterica-searching shopping trips.

Kate makes shopping fun and cool. Thank you Kate. You are a nice person.

Consider it the doppelganger version of The Consumerist as she:

1) Enjoys shopping.
2) Puts up original content instead of mainly remixing and pissing on the work of others.
3) Has a site both visually pleasing and enjoyable to navigate.

Visit ObsessiveConsumption.com.
