Savvy saving and style pointers for around the home.

  • Use sea shells for a unique laundry soap scoop.

  • Rusty Shirts: Soak in equal parts lemon juice and water

  • Sweat-Stained Shirts: Before washing, sprinkle meat tenderizer on the stains and rub in gently. Or, soak in a quart of water with a handful of salt for an hour.

  • Mount a ladder as a shelving unit for books.

  • Faces can double as a cheap and fun chair. Easy store n' hide when not in use.
