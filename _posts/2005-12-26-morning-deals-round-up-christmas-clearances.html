The Consumerist is technically off today, but we're doing a bit of Boxing Day online browsing ourselves and thought we'd let you know what's shaking, deal-wise.

• Gamefly, the 'Netflix for Games,' are selling copies of their used games for $3 off their already usually discounted price, plus free shipping. We have bought games before from Gamefly and found them to be nearly flawless—and there's a guaranteee from Gamefly if they are not. Take a look.

• Buy.com has after Christmas clearance on electronics, computers, and DVDs.
