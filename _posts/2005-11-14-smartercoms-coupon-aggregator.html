Hot Deals sites are a dime a dozen, but the deals are almost always for junk you didn't actually plan on buying. How does one discover discounts on products one actually wants to buy? The coupon page at Smarter.com is one stop.

At Smarter, you can browse by store, to try and find a coupon for a product you've already got in your cart. Or browse by category, and tailor your purchase to the store offering the deepest discount.

Is the Smarter coupon site the most comprehensive coupon aggregator around? We have no clue—we just started using it ourselves. If you've got a better option, we'd love to hear about it.
