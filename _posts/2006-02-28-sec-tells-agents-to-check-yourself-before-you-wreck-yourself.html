Securities and Exchange Commission chief Chris Cox reprimanded the agency
s enforcement division for subpoenaing two journalists, declaring their behavior renegade.

The smackdown was as rare a move as the subpoena was in the first place. The injunctions filed against the journalists are in conjunction with accusations that they conspired with short sellers to downgrade company
s stock, including Overstock.com. The online retailer is suing the reporters for slander and libel. The CEO of that company, Patrick Byrne, henceforth known as The Jabberwock, has been recorded as making abusive and inflammatory remarks against the jourmalists.
